Shop the Latest Women's Jewellery Designs Online


The right jewellery is an essential part of every great attire. Most women have an affinity
towards wearing jewellery that compliments their looks and their sense of style. That is why
women need to find the perfect traditional or fashion jewelry that reflects their style statement
and helps them accessorize their outfits better. With that thought in mind, sisters Mamta and
Vidhi Gupta founded Zariin, a boutique jewellery store that speaks to the everyday style and
sensibility of modern women. Perfectly balancing fashion and functionality since its inception in
2010, Zariin is among the most popular and loved online jewelry stores in the country.


A testament to the outstanding quality and craftsmanship of Zariin jewellery, we have been
recognized internationally including the prestigious GRAZIA Young Fashion Award for
excellence in Accessory Design in 2013. The ELLE Graduate in Jewellery has also been
awarded to Zariin jewelry store and it has also been among the top 5 performers in
Swarovski’s Confluence, an intiative by Swarovski to partner with India’s top designer labels.
Along with awards, the list of celebrities who have shown admiration of the brand include
Sonam Kapoor, Deepika Padukone, Priyanka Chopra, Nargis Fakhri, Chitrangda Singh, Soha
Ali Khan, Freida Pinto, Kalki Koechlin and stars of hit TV series – All my children, and Gossip
Girl. This is made possible by expert craftsmanship of our skilled in-house artisans after each
jewel being sketched and imagined by our jewellery designers. All of this translates to making
Zariin an outstanding online jewellery shopping destination.
